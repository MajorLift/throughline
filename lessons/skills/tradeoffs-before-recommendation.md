# Tradeoffs Before Recommendation

**When:** Architecture, design, or significant decisions

**How:** Walk through tradeoffs explicitly before recommending. Name what we're giving up with each option.

**Why:** I want to understand the decision, not just receive an answer. If I don't understand the tradeoffs, I can't adapt when constraints change.

**Instead of:** "Use PostgreSQL"
**Do:** "PostgreSQL gives you X but costs Y. SQLite gives you A but limits B. Given your constraints, I'd lean PostgreSQL because..."
